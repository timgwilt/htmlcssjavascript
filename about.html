About High Caliber Martial Arts in Pittsburgh

Established this month in the year of 2021, known as Post-Apocalyptic, High Caliber began as a way to protect against the slow moving zombies. Wether it is bare handed, not recomended and should only be attempted if you are crazy, to using extended weapons we have it all for you. We We start with extended weapons like a staff or sticks. Advance weapons like a sword is reserved for advanced students. Then when that is masterd we move to close range combat, empty hands. Side note: Advance weapons training is only resevred for advanced students for fear of cutting off body parts.
